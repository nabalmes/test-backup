The Luks Lofts Hotel Standard

Luks Lofts Hotel is the only 5-star hotel in Batangas City, Philippines that uses state-of-the-art technology in providing luxury hospitality and an upscaled guest experience.

Committed to providing luxury hospitality experience

We have 10 innovative lofts you can luxuriate in. Each has its own jet pool and sauna for a more private and enlivening luxury hospitality experience. Our rooms are operated through a secure premise-based technology designed and controlled in-house.

Upon check-in, you will be assigned a Luks Device. Order room service, control your room lights, or request for an in-room massage through our Digital Butler.

Everything you need is just a tap away. Our tech is paired with exceptionally trained envoys guided by principles of honesty, integrity, respect, and dedication to service.

Committed to wellness

We value the importance of private spaces and tranquility for a more relaxing and rejuvenating stay.

Apart from jet pools and saunas which you can enjoy right in the comforts of your loft, Luks Spa offers a wide range of world-class treatments.

Heal and recharge to your heart's desire.

Committed to sustainability

We believe that we are responsible for future generations. We acknowledge our three pillars in promoting sustainability:

  • Our Technology
  • Our Partners
  • Our Guests

Our Guests Our lofts are equipped with energy-saving devices and motion sensor fixtures to lessen our impact on the environment.

Our partners are trained in environment-friendly hospitality practices that facilitate efficient service.
Our guests are encouraged to reduce, reuse, and recycle.

The New Normal

COVID19 continues and we are moving towards our new normal. Luks Lofts Hotel remains steadfast and proactive in complying with rules and regulations to prevent the spread of the virus.

As we welcome you back to our lofts, we are more committed to creating luxuriously safe and private spaces with our safety protocols.

Safety First

  • Mask & Physical Distancing
    Face masks are required for all envoys and guests in the hotel's public spaces.
  • Constant Disinfection of Surface Areas and Public Spaces
    Rigorous and frequent disinfection of surfaces are done using hospital-grade cleaning products. Sprayers amd air purifiers are likewise strategically plcaed in various areas.
  • Flexible Stays
    We understand that these are challenging times. Flexible stays and rebookings are provided for your utmost convenience.
  • Clean and Safe Lofts
    Your loft is thoroughly cleaned and sanitized using hospital-grade disinfectants. Additional disinfection is also in place during the laundry process of your bathrobe, towel, handcloth, and bed linen. A stringent disinfection of your jetpool and sauna is also performed.
  • Love for technology
    As part of Luks Lofts Hotel's promise of providing luxury hospitality, we advance the use of technology in various touch points during your stay to lessen face-to-face contact.
  • Weekly Rapid Test
    Our envoys are tested weekly to alleviate the risk of any possible transmission.
